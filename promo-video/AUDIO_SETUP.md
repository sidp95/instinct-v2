# Audio Files Required

Place the following audio files in the `public/audio/` folder:

## Required Files:

1. **trap-beat.mp3** - Background trap/hip-hop beat with a hard drop
   - Should be energetic and hype
   - The beat drop should hit right at the start
   - Duration: ~20 seconds

2. **buzzer-cheer.mp3** - Stadium buzzer + crowd cheer
   - Loud buzzer sound followed by crowd cheering
   - Duration: ~2-3 seconds

3. **swoosh.mp3** - Swipe/swoosh sound effect
   - Quick swoosh sound for card swipes
   - Duration: ~0.5 seconds

4. **coins.mp3** - Coins clinking/stacking sound
   - Sound of coins dropping/stacking
   - Duration: ~1-2 seconds

## Where to get free sound effects:

- **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/
- **Freesound** - https://freesound.org/
- **Mixkit** - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/

## For the trap beat:

- **Pixabay Music** - https://pixabay.com/music/search/trap/
- **YouTube Audio Library** (if you have access)

## Once you have the files:

1. Place them in `promo-video/public/audio/`
2. Restart Remotion studio: `npm run start`
3. Preview the video at http://localhost:3001
